Matter

Matter is made up of atoms, the smallest particles of an element.

A vacuum is an area containing no matter.

Plasma can be found in polar auroras.

The volume of gas is not constant.

Matter has inter particle space.

Particles in matter are always in state of motion.

The motion of the particles increases with an increase in tempature.

The three types of matter are Solids, Liquids, and Gases.

Solid matter is hard, it has shape and volume.
